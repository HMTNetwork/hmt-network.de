# Ranks on HMT-Network

Ranks give you exclusive benefits across the entire network.

## [ + ] Plus Rank
The Plus rank offers useful benefits for everyday play:
* **Lobby Flight:** Use `/fly` in the lobbies to move faster.

## [ * ] Star Rank
The Star rank is our highest rank with maximum freedom:
* **Speed:** Use `/speed` on all servers to adjust your walking speed.
* **All Benefits:** Naturally includes all functions of the Plus rank.
